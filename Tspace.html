TSpace

Bringing students with learning disabilites a chance with learning.

TSpace is a network where various students with certain learning disabilities discover teachers suitable for their learning style. Educationally challenged students can browse teachers who have capabilities of teaching them in a school environment. They can contact the needed mentor if for any questions that may assist them in further school application plans, giving these students another shot in learning in the school environment.
