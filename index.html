TSpace: The Cogs and Screws (How It Works)

Users that go the website may enter a username as they open the website. Students or teachers may check out the profiles of people documented on the website, and under their profiles are additional information (subject taught, contact number, country, etc) that users may use to contact the selected person. With this, students can find the right teachers for them, and pave way to school application opportunities, which can benefit the student into joining an educational institution with the teacher's help.

The Arising Issue: Lack of Accesible Learning

Many children around the world come from different backgrounds and lifestyles, and some of which are based on disorders or disabilities acquired by the student, and unfortunately, these conditions may heavily affect their progress in learning. One of the effects of these backgrounds can be their hindrance to a student’s ability to adapt to the general style of learning.

Our Goal

This leads to our goal to help these students to find the right teachers for them which would willingly help and direct them to access the same education given to other students.

Our Purpose

This website aims to guide students in finding the right teacher that would assist them in their path in learning. It provides a system where students can survey various teachers logged in to the website, and may communicate with them if they are interested in learning under their care. Vice versa, the teachers may also find students who they find applicable for their style of teaching, and assist them in learning with other peers.
